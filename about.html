About

Senior Product Designer

SaaS · Marketplaces · Internal tools

I design complex products end to end — support platforms, e-commerce, internal tools, and AI-powered features — turning tangled workflows into things people understand and actually use.

Most of my career has been at Ubisoft, owning UX for tools that serve millions of players a year. Lately I've been building AI into the way products work and the way I design them — including shipping my own AI product.
