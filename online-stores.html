What I do.

I build Shopify stores end to end — the theme, products and collections, checkout, payments, and everything needed to start selling. You handle the business; I handle the technical side, and show you how to run it when it's done. Two kinds of stores, same care either way:

Shopify for dropshippers

Your supplier holds the inventory and ships the orders. I connect the supplier apps to Shopify and organise the product pages so a raw supplier listing becomes a clear, properly structured page — not a dump of specs.

Shopify for product owners

You make or stock your own products. I set up the catalog, variants, and collections, with a clean checkout and the structure to keep a growing range organised as you add to it.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know anything technical?

No. I set up Shopify, the products, the checkout, payments, and any supplier apps. When it's live, I walk you through adding products and handling orders so you're comfortable running it.

Can you set up a dropshipping store?

Yes. I connect your supplier and fulfillment apps to Shopify and organise the product pages so supplier listings become proper, structured pages. No stock or warehouse on your side.

Why Shopify rather than something cheaper?

For selling products, Shopify keeps checkout, payments, inventory, and apps in one place, which means fewer things to break. If your business doesn't sell products and just needs a site, the Business Websites option is usually cheaper to run.

What does it cost to keep the store running?

Beyond my work: a domain (small yearly fee), your Shopify plan (monthly), and standard payment-processing fees per sale. I'll go through the real numbers for your case before you commit.

Do I get a custom design, or a theme?

The build uses a well-configured theme so you can start selling. A fully custom design is an optional add-on if you want the store to look distinct.

Can I manage products and orders myself afterwards?

Yes. Onboarding is included so you'll know how. If you'd rather hand it off or learn the deeper parts later, ongoing support is available.
