Process

How I work

01 / Understand

Getting to the root of the problem through research, conversations, and whatever data already exists. AI speeds up the messy part — synthesizing notes, spotting patterns — leaving more room for actual thinking.

02 / Design

Moving from flows to high‑fidelity UI, proposing a few directions rather than betting on one. AI helps generate and pressure‑test variations; deciding which one holds up stays a design decision.

03 / Design Delivery

Turning the chosen direction into finished, build‑ready design — clear files, organized components, and a handoff teams can work from. You'll always know what's coming and where things stand.
