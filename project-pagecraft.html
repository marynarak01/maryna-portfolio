Step 1 · Product input

Paste a URL, or describe the product.

Give Pagecraft a product URL, or describe the product with text and photos. It reads the page and analyzes the product — no manual setup.

Step 2 · Strategy

It works out the angle for you.

Pagecraft builds a buyer profile — motivation, awareness, objections — then picks the content angle that fits, scored in the same analysis pass. Every field is editable, or skip it and use the AI default. The strategy is decided for the user, not supplied by them.

Step 3 · Layout

The right sections, in the right order.

A layout is chosen to fit the product — section order fully editable, with industry presets (food, skincare, tools, apparel, services, SaaS, games, books) baked in. Skippable, and entirely in the user's hands.

Step 4 · Generate

The full page, written to that angle.

Every section is generated in order and written to the strategy — title and CTA, problem framing, benefits, features, specs, what's-in-the-box, how-to-use, FAQ, testimonials, guarantee, final CTA — plus SEO meta. Refine any section inline. The output is copy-ready.
