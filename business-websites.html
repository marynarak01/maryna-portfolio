What I do.

Most small-business owners don't have the time or the technical background to build and run a website themselves — and they shouldn't have to. I take it on end to end: the design, the build, connecting things like a booking form or a reservation system, and setting up the domain and hosting if you want me to. When it's ready, I show you how it works so you're not dependent on anyone to make small changes.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know anything technical to take care of my website afterwards?

No. I set everything up, and once it's live I show you how to handle the everyday changes. You won't need to deal with the technical side.

How long does it take?

It depends on the site — how big it is and how quickly you can get me your content and photos. I'll give you a realistic timeline once I understand the scope.

So I pay you for the work, but I also pay for something else?

Yes. My fee covers building the site. Separately, there's the platform that keeps it online — hosting on WordPress, or a monthly plan on Webflow — and your domain. Those are paid to the providers, not to me, and they stay yours.

Can I update the site myself afterwards?

Yes — a walkthrough is included, so you'll know how to make everyday changes. If you'd like ongoing help with your site beyond that, we can agree a price for it.

I already have a website. Can you fix it?

Often yes, sometimes no — it depends on what's there and what you need. Send it over and I'll tell you honestly.
